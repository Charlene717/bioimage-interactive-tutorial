03 精選 spotlight：2025–2026 SOTA

NEW 2025

Cellpose 4 (Cellpose-SAM)

將 SAM transformer backbone 與 Cellpose 流場 head 結合，單一模型橫掃多種顯微術式，幾乎不需要再 fine-tune。Pachitariu et al., bioRxiv 2025。

Combines a SAM transformer backbone with the Cellpose flow-field head — one model across modalities, almost no fine-tuning needed. Pachitariu et al., bioRxiv 2025.

NEW 2024

JUMP-CP: 3M Cell Painting Images

3,072,156 張影像 × 12,602 化合物 + 8,069 基因擾動，由 Broad 領導的 12 家機構聯盟發表，是迄今最大規模的公開表型篩選資料集。Chandrasekaran et al., Nature Methods 21:1114–1121。

3,072,156 images × 12,602 compounds + 8,069 genetic perturbations — the largest public phenotypic screen ever, from a 12-institution Broad-led consortium. Chandrasekaran et al., Nature Methods 21:1114–1121.

NEW 2025

Micro-SAM (µSAM) for Microscopy

針對光學 + 電子顯微鏡 fine-tune 的 SAM 變體，napari plugin 提示式分割，2D / 3D / 影片皆可。Archit et al., Nature Methods 22:579–591。

SAM fine-tuned on light + EM microscopy, with a napari plugin for promptable segmentation — 2D / 3D / video. Archit et al., Nature Methods 22:579–591.

04 使用提示

💡 看圖先於設參數所有的閾值、半徑、min_distance 都要先看 violin / scatter / histogram 再決定。沒有「放諸四海皆準」的數字。Every threshold, radius, and min_distance starts with a violin / scatter / histogram. No magic numbers work universally.
💡 第一步永遠是 print shape讀檔後立刻 print(img.shape, img.dtype, dims, pixel_size)。90% bug 在這行被抓出來。Right after loading: print(img.shape, img.dtype, dims, pixel_size). 90% of bugs surface here.
💡 Pretrained 先試零訓練Cellpose-SAM cyto3、StarDist versatile_fluo 通常零訓練就夠用。動手訓練前先讓 generalist 跑一遍。Cellpose-SAM cyto3, StarDist versatile_fluo often suffice zero-shot. Run a generalist before training anything.
💡 Anisotropy 是 3D #1 陷阱共軛焦 Z 通常比 XY 大 3–10 倍。不告訴模型 anisotropy = 細胞被「壓扁」的 silent failure。Confocal Z is 3–10× coarser than XY. Without anisotropy=, cells get silently "flattened."
💡 量化前不要任意濾波Gaussian 會改變強度峰值與共定位係數。必要時記錄參數並一致套用於對照組。Gaussian skews peaks & colocalization. Document parameters and apply identically across conditions if you must.
💡 用獨立 test set 評估「眼睛看訓練集」永遠樂觀。算 IoU / Dice / AP / TRA / DET 才有可信數字。Eyeballing the training set is always optimistic. Quantify IoU/Dice/AP/TRA/DET on a held-out test set.
