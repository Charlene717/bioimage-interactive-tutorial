Bioimage Analysis 互動式教學

從像素到生物學洞見——13 個主題帶你掌握生物影像分析的核心流程：從顯微影像基礎、預處理、分割、追蹤、量化，到深度學習與基礎模型的最新進展。

From pixels to biological insights — master bioimage analysis in 13 modules: microscopy fundamentals, preprocessing, segmentation, tracking, quantification, and the latest deep-learning and foundation-model advances.

01 核心分析流程

🖼️
Step 1

影像基礎

像素 / 體素、bit depth、通道、顯微術式、TIFF / OME-TIFF / CZI / LIF、Bio-Formats、OME-Zarr。

Pixels/voxels, bit depth, channels, microscopy modalities, TIFF/OME-TIFF/CZI/LIF, Bio-Formats, OME-Zarr.
🧽
Step 2

預處理與去噪

Gaussian / median / BM3D、rolling-ball、BaSiC 平場校正、Noise2Void、Cellpose3 一鍵還原。

Gaussian/median/BM3D, rolling-ball, BaSiC shading, Noise2Void, Cellpose3 one-click restoration.
🔍
Step 3

去卷積

PSF 模型、Richardson-Lucy、Wiener、CARE / DeepStorm 深度學習還原，反轉顯微鏡的光學模糊。

PSF models, Richardson-Lucy, Wiener, CARE/DeepStorm deep restoration — reverse optical blur.
🧭
Step 4

配準與拼接

剛體 / 仿射 / B-spline 變換、互資訊、BigStitcher 大樣本拼接、ANTs SyN 形變對齊。

Rigid/affine/B-spline transforms, mutual information, BigStitcher tile fusion, ANTs SyN.
✂️
Step 5

傳統分割

Otsu / Li / Triangle 閾值、形態學運算、距離變換、Watershed、regionprops，scikit-image 核心套件。

Otsu/Li/Triangle thresholding, morphology, distance transform, watershed, regionprops via scikit-image.
🧠
Step 6

深度學習分割

U-Net、Cellpose 4 (Cellpose-SAM)、StarDist、nnU-Net、Mesmer、Omnipose，零訓練泛化模型。

U-Net, Cellpose 4 (Cellpose-SAM), StarDist, nnU-Net, Mesmer, Omnipose — generalist pretrained models.
🌐
Step 7

AI 基礎模型

SAM / SAM 2、Micro-SAM、MedSAM2、CellSAM、UNI / Virchow 病理基礎模型，提示式分割新範式。

SAM/SAM 2, Micro-SAM, MedSAM2, CellSAM, UNI/Virchow pathology FMs — promptable segmentation.
🛰️
Step 8

細胞追蹤

LAP / Kalman、TrackMate、btrack、Trackastra Transformer 追蹤、譜系樹、Cell Tracking Challenge 指標。

LAP/Kalman, TrackMate, btrack, Trackastra Transformer, lineage trees, CTC metrics.
📐
Step 9

量化與特徵

強度統計、形態學、共定位 (Pearson / Manders / Costes)、Haralick 紋理、PyRadiomics、Cell Painting。

Intensity, morphology, colocalization (Pearson/Manders/Costes), Haralick texture, PyRadiomics.
