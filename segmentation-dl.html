深度學習分割

傳統方法處理不了相黏細胞、不規則形狀、複雜組織。DL 分割器現在能跨顯微術式泛化，幾乎不用重訓——已是細胞 / 核 / 組織區段的預設首選。

Classical methods fail on touching cells, irregular morphologies, and crowded tissue. DL segmenters now generalize across modalities with little or no retraining — the default for cells, nuclei, and tissue compartments.

開場故事 · STORY

U-Net 2015：被審稿人說「沒新意」的曠世論文

2015 年 Ronneberger 等投稿 MICCAI 的 U-Net 論文，據傳審稿人之一寫「another encoder-decoder, not novel」差點被拒。十年後 U-Net 被引 100,000+ 次，是醫學影像 DL 最被引論文，從 CNN 到 transformer 後代仍以它為骨幹。

2020 Stringer 等把 U-Net 改造成「流場輸出」的 Cellpose；2022 加入 human-in-the-loop；2025 Cellpose-SAM 把 SAM ViT backbone 接上 Cellpose head，單一通用模型橫掃多種顯微術式。零訓練泛化的時代來臨。

Ronneberger's 2015 U-Net submission to MICCAI reportedly had a reviewer write 'another encoder-decoder, not novel' and almost got rejected. Ten years later, U-Net has 100,000+ citations, the most-cited paper in medical-imaging DL — every CNN-to-transformer descendant still uses it as backbone.

In 2020 Stringer's Cellpose recast U-Net as 'flow-field output'; 2022 added human-in-the-loop; 2025 Cellpose-SAM swapped in a SAM ViT backbone with Cellpose's head, one generalist model dominating most microscopy modalities. The zero-shot generalization era has arrived.

觀念

一、語意 / 實例 / 全景

🎨 語意

每個像素分到一個類別（細胞 / 背景 / 細胞核 / 細胞質）。不分個體。例：U-Net 輸出。

Per-pixel class label (cell / background / nucleus / cytoplasm). No individual identity. E.g., U-Net output.

🔢 實例

每個物件一個唯一 ID（cell 1, cell 2 …）。是下游所有量化的前提。Cellpose, StarDist, Mask R-CNN 都輸出實例分割。

Each object has a unique ID (cell 1, cell 2 …). Prerequisite for downstream quantification. Cellpose, StarDist, Mask R-CNN output instances.

🎭 全景

語意 + 實例混合。「可數物件」(thing) 用實例分；背景區域 (stuff) 用語意分。多細胞組織分析常見。

Semantic + instance combined. "Things" get instance IDs; "stuff" (background regions) gets semantic labels. Common in multi-cell tissue analysis.

三、選哪個模型？

🌳 DL 分割選擇樹

Q1:
是密集圓核（如組織切片 DAPI）？→ StarDist 2D_versatile_fluo 或 StarDist H&E
Q2:
是一般細胞（活細胞、培養皿）？→ Cellpose-SAM (cyto3 模型)；先零訓練試試
Q3:
是多重染色組織（CODEX/IMC）？→ Mesmer（記得 channel 0 = nucleus, channel 1 = membrane）

Q5:
是 H&E 病理 / 醫學影像（CT/MRI），且需自訂任務？→ nnU-Net（自配置，要花時間訓練）
Q6:
所有 pretrained 都不行？→ 收集 50–100 張標註，Cellpose human-in-the-loop fine-tune（GUI 友好）或 nnU-Net

Densely packed round nuclei (DAPI in tissue)? → StarDist 2D_versatile_fluo or StarDist H&E
Q2:
General cells (live, culture)? → Cellpose-SAM (cyto3 model); try zero-shot first

💡
原則：先試 pretrained 通用模型零訓練（Cellpose-SAM cyto3、StarDist versatile）。大多數人從來不需要自己訓練。
真要訓練時，先用少量標註 + human-in-the-loop，再考慮全量訓練。 Principle: Try a pretrained generalist (Cellpose-SAM cyto3, StarDist versatile) zero-shot first. Most users never train.
When you must train, start with a few labels + human-in-the-loop before full training.
評估

四、分割結果評估

  • IoU (Intersection over Union, Jaccard)：交集 / 聯集，0–1 越高越好
  • Dice (F1)：2·交集 / (預測+真值)，與 IoU 同調但更敏感
  • AP@IoU：物件層級平均精度，IoU 閾值不同會得不同分數 (常見 0.5, 0.75)
  • Panoptic Quality (PQ)：偵測 + 分割綜合分數
  • Cell Tracking Challenge SEG：每個 GT 物件與最大重疊預測的 IoU 平均

務必在從未見過的測試集上評估。在訓練集上看「眼睛感覺準」是學習曲線一定會遇到的陷阱。

  • IoU (Jaccard): intersection over union, 0–1, higher is better
  • Dice (F1): 2·intersection / (pred + GT), more sensitive than IoU
  • AP@IoU: object-level average precision at given IoU thresholds (commonly 0.5, 0.75)
  • Panoptic Quality (PQ): combined detection + segmentation score
  • Cell Tracking Challenge SEG: mean IoU between each GT and best-overlap prediction

Always evaluate on a held-out test set. "It looks right by eye on the training set" is a trap every learner falls into once.

公式拆解

Cellpose 流場運算

Σ 逐步推導
(dY, dX) = ∇centroid(image)
① CNN 預測CNN prediction U-Net 輸出每個像素的 (dY, dX) 向量（指向細胞中心）+ cell probability。U-Net outputs each pixel's (dY, dX) vector (pointing to the cell centre) + cell probability.
② 歐拉積分Euler integration 每像素沿流場「走 N 步」: x_new = x + Δt · (dY, dX)；通常 N≈200 步。Each pixel walks N steps along the flow: x_new = x + Δt · (dY, dX); usually N≈200.
③ 群聚收斂Clustering convergence 收斂到同一個 attractor (sink) 的像素 → 同一個 cell ID。Pixels converging to the same attractor (sink) → same cell ID.
④ 過濾Filter 非物件像素流向圖框外（pseudo-attractor）；用 cellprob_threshold 過濾。Non-object pixels flow off-frame (pseudo-attractor); filter with cellprob_threshold.
比 semantic mask 多了「跑到中心」的物理直覺，因此對相黏細胞特別擅長。Cellpose-SAM 把流場 head 接到 SAM ViT-H backbone 後，泛化能力再提升一個量級。
Adds the 'flow to centre' physical intuition over semantic masks — excellent at touching cells. Cellpose-SAM connects this head to a SAM ViT-H backbone, lifting generalization another order of magnitude.
思辨討論

DL 分割三大爭論

A. StarDist 對神經元為何不好？
你試 StarDist 分割神經元 axon——結果只抓到 cell body。為什麼？
You tried StarDist on neurons — only the cell body got picked up. Why?
提示：StarDist 假設物件為 star-convex（從中心放射，無凹陷）。神經元有長軸與分支，違反假設。改用 Omnipose 或 Cellpose-SAM。
Hint: StarDist assumes star-convexity (no concavities). Neurons have axons and branches — breaks the assumption. Use Omnipose or Cellpose-SAM.
B. Cellpose-SAM 真能取代 Mesmer？
Mesmer 專為 CODEX / IMC 設計，cyto3 號稱泛化。實務上你怎麼選？
Mesmer is built for CODEX/IMC; cyto3 claims to generalize. How do you choose in practice?
提示：multiplex tissue 仍建議 Mesmer (channel-order 嚴格、訓練資料專一)；其他情境 Cellpose-SAM 為通用首選。
Hint: for multiplex tissue, prefer Mesmer (strict channel order, dedicated training data); otherwise Cellpose-SAM is the generalist default.
C. 標多少 mask 才夠 fine-tune？
你的特殊樣本 Cellpose-SAM 零訓練不夠好。你會標幾張？
Your sample doesn't work zero-shot. How many do you label?

精選案例

兩個重塑分割的故事

① Cellpose human-in-the-loop
Pachitariu et al., Nat Methods 19:1634 (2022) · cellpose.org GUI

2022 年 Cellpose 2.0 在 Nat Methods 引入 human-in-the-loop fine-tuning GUI：跑零訓練 → 用戶修正錯誤 → 模型自動 fine-tune → 再跑。整個循環 5 分鐘內可重複，50 張影像即可訓練出 SOTA 領域模型。Stringer Lab 把 ML 變成「拖滑鼠」——讓沒有任何 DL 背景的生物學家也能訓練自己的分割器。

Cellpose 2.0 (Nat Methods 2022) introduced a human-in-the-loop fine-tuning GUI: run zero-shot → user corrects errors → model auto-fine-tunes → run again. The whole loop is repeatable in 5 minutes, and 50 labelled images suffice to train a SOTA domain model. Stringer Lab turned ML into 'dragging a mouse' — biologists with zero DL background train their own segmenters.

啟示：好工具不只強大，更要讓非專家也能用。
Lesson: great tools aren't just powerful — they're usable by non-experts.
② nnU-Net 連刷 BraTS 五年第一
Isensee et al., Nat Methods 18:203 (2021) · BraTS / KiTS / Medical Segmentation Decathlon

Fabian Isensee 等 2021 在 Nat Methods 發表 nnU-Net：核心理念是 「don't tune; auto-configure」——自動決定預處理、3D vs 2D、loss、optimizer。結果：在 BraTS（腦腫瘤）、KiTS（腎臟）、Medical Decathlon 等十多項挑戰連續多年第一。證明「精心調的 baseline > 花俏的新架構」。

Isensee et al. (Nat Methods 2021) released nnU-Net with one credo: 'don't tune; auto-configure.' The framework auto-decides preprocessing, 2D vs 3D, loss, optimizer. Result: top-1 on BraTS, KiTS, the Medical Decathlon, and a dozen other challenges for years. Proof that 'a well-tuned baseline beats fancy new architectures.'

啟示：別搶著發新模型，先把現有的好好調。
Lesson: don't rush to publish a new model — tune the existing one properly first.
常見陷阱

DL 分割六大陷阱

P1Mesmer 通道順序顛倒
ch0 應為核、ch1 為膜；順序錯不報錯但完全分割錯誤。
ch0=nucleus, ch1=membrane; swapping silently breaks segmentation.
讀檔後印 channel_names 並肉眼對照。
Always print channel_names and visually verify.
P2訓練集眼睛驗證
訓練集看起來「完美」，獨立 test set 卻爆炸。
Looks perfect on training set, explodes on the held-out test.
保留 20% 為 test set；在它上面算 IoU/AP。
Hold out 20% for testing; compute IoU/AP there.
P3小資料過度 fit
只 30 張影像就跑 500 epochs，模型背下訓練集。
30 images × 500 epochs → the model memorizes training set.
Augmentation + 早停 + transfer learning。
Use augmentation, early stopping, and transfer learning.
P4忽略 anisotropy 在 3D
Cellpose 3D 不傳 anisotropy → cell 變扁。
Cellpose 3D without anisotropy= → flattened cells.
傳 anisotropy=dz/dxy；或先 resample 到 isotropic。
Pass anisotropy=dz/dxy or resample to isotropic first.
P5Dice 0.95 = 完美？
小物件 missing 10% 也可能 Dice 仍 0.95（被大物件主導）。
Even 10% missing small objects can keep Dice at 0.95 (dominated by large objects).
算 per-object F1、AP@0.5/0.75；用 Cell Tracking Challenge SEG。
Compute per-object F1, AP@0.5/0.75; use CTC SEG metric.
P6盲目訓練自己模型
沒先試 pretrained generalist 就重訓。
Retrain without first trying a pretrained generalist.
先試 Cellpose-SAM cyto3 / StarDist versatile；不夠才訓練。
Try Cellpose-SAM cyto3 / StarDist versatile first; only retrain if needed.
