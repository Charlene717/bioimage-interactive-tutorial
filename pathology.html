ADVANCED

數位病理

病理玻片掃成 Giga-pixel 金字塔影像 (WSI)，撐起癌症診斷、生物標誌探索與臨床 AI。Tile → 染色正規化 → 推論 → slide 級聚合，是這個領域獨特的工作流。

Pathology slides are scanned as gigapixel pyramidal images (WSI) — the foundation of cancer diagnostics, biomarker discovery, and clinical AI. Tile → stain normalize → per-tile inference → slide-level aggregation is this domain's signature workflow.

開場故事 · STORY

從 1838 細胞理論到 2024 病理 foundation model：186 年

1838 年 Schleiden / Schwann 在染色木屑上看見「細胞理論」開啟了組織學；1986 年 Aperio 推出第一台 whole-slide scanner，玻片變成 giga-pixel 影像；2017 年 Peter Bankhead 一個人在 Belfast 寫完 QuPath，把 IHC 分析從 ImageJ macro 解放出來。

2024 年 UNI / Virchow / CONCH 三大病理基礎模型登場，slide-level 分類進入 foundation-model 時代。一張 H&E 切片有 80,000×80,000 pixel，背後是一整個世紀的方法演進。

Schleiden and Schwann's 1838 cell theory (from stained wood shavings) opened histology; Aperio's 1986 whole-slide scanner turned glass slides into gigapixel images; Peter Bankhead single-handedly wrote QuPath in Belfast in 2017, freeing IHC analysis from ImageJ macros.

In 2024 the three pathology foundation models — UNI, Virchow, CONCH — arrived; slide-level classification entered the foundation-model era. One H&E slide is 80,000×80,000 pixels, and behind it sits a century of methodological evolution.

WSI 基礎

一、WSI 格式

WSI = Whole-Slide Image，整張病理切片掃成的金字塔影像：

  • 一張切片約 20×20 mm，40× 掃描 = 80k × 80k pixel ≈ 數 GB
  • 金字塔：原解析度（level 0）+ 縮小版（level 1, 2 …）
  • 用 tile（512×512 或 256×256） 處理，不會整檔載入

常見格式：

  • .svs Aperio（最常見）、.ndpi Hamamatsu、.mrxs 3DHistech
  • .dcm DICOM-WSI（醫療標準）
  • Microns Per Pixel (MPP) 是關鍵 metadata：20× ≈ 0.5 µm/px，40× ≈ 0.25 µm/px

WSI = Whole-Slide Image — a pathology slide digitized as a pyramidal image:

  • ~20×20 mm slide at 40× = 80k × 80k pixels ≈ several GB
  • Pyramid: native resolution (level 0) + downsampled levels (1, 2, …)
  • Processed in tiles (512×512 or 256×256) — never loaded whole

Common formats:

  • .svs Aperio (most common), .ndpi Hamamatsu, .mrxs 3DHistech
  • .dcm DICOM-WSI (medical standard)
  • Microns Per Pixel (MPP) is the critical metadata: 20× ≈ 0.5 µm/px, 40× ≈ 0.25 µm/px
染色正規化

二、染色正規化

不同醫院 / 染色批次 / 掃描儀的 H&E 顏色差異極大——粉、紫、橘、藍。DL 模型對顏色非常敏感，跨機構部署必須正規化。

方法原理特色
ReinhardLAB 空間配對 mean / std最簡單；對組織量不平衡敏感Match LAB-space mean/stdSimplest; sensitive to tissue-fraction imbalance
Macenko 2009在 OD 空間找 H 與 E 兩 stain 向量事實標準，TIA Toolbox、QuPath 預設Find H&E stain vectors in OD spaceDe facto standard; default in TIA Toolbox, QuPath
Vahadane 2016SNMF 分離 stain，更貼近生物更準；較慢Sparse NMF stain separationMore faithful; slower
StainGAN / StainNetGAN / CNN 學跨機構對映SOTA；需訓練資料GAN/CNN learning cross-site mappingSOTA; needs training data

H&E colour varies enormously across hospitals/batches/scanners — pink, purple, orange, blue. DL models are highly colour-sensitive; cross-site deployment demands normalization.

DL pipeline

三、WSI DL 工作流

組織偵測

用 HSV 飽和度或預訓練 GrandQC 偵測組織區域，排除背景白色 → 大幅減少 tile 數量。

Detect tissue via HSV saturation or pretrained GrandQC — drop background tiles to slash tile count.

Tile 切割

在指定 MPP 切 256×256 或 512×512 patch。對齊 pretrained 模型期望的 MPP（多數 ~0.5 µm/px = 20×）。

Extract 256×256 or 512×512 patches at a target MPP — match what your pretrained model expects (often 0.5 µm/px ≈ 20×).

染色正規化 + tile 推論

每 tile 跑分割 (HoVer-Net, StarDist H&E, CellViT) 或分類 (UNI/Virchow embedding + classifier)。

Per tile: segmentation (HoVer-Net, StarDist H&E, CellViT) or classification (UNI/Virchow embedding + classifier).

Slide-level 聚合

幾千個 tile 的 embedding → slide-level 預測。Multiple Instance Learning：ABMIL、CLAM、TransMIL、DSMIL。CLAM 是教學首選。

Thousands of tile embeddings → slide-level prediction. Multiple Instance Learning: ABMIL, CLAM, TransMIL, DSMIL. CLAM is the teaching default.

解釋性

CLAM 等 attention MIL 輸出 per-tile 注意力分數 → 在 slide 上畫熱圖，告訴病理醫師「模型看哪裡」。

Attention MIL outputs per-tile attention → heatmap overlaid on the slide, showing pathologists "where the model looks."

⚠️
放大倍率陷阱：UNI / Virchow 等 FM 預期 ~0.5 µm/px。若你直接餵 40× (0.25 µm/px)，性能會悄悄下降。讀 slide.properties["openslide.mpp-x"] 並做正確的 level 選擇。 Magnification trap: UNI / Virchow expect ~0.5 µm/px. Feeding 40× (0.25 µm/px) directly silently hurts performance. Read slide.properties["openslide.mpp-x"] and pick the right level.

公式拆解

Macenko 染色正規化步驟

Σ 逐步推導
OD = −log10(I / I0)
① RGB → ODRGB → OD 所有像素轉到光學密度空間，stain 在此線性疊加。Convert every pixel to OD space — stains are linear there.
② PCA on ODPCA on OD 在 OD 空間做 PCA 取前 2 個主成分（對應 H 與 E 軸）。PCA in OD space — top 2 components correspond to H and E axes.
③ 百分位數定軸Percentile axes 在主成分平面取 1%, 99% 角度，定 H / E stain vector。Take 1% and 99% angles in the PC plane to define H / E stain vectors.
④ 投影到參考Project to reference 把目標影像 stain vector 投影到參考 slide 的 stain space。Project target stain vectors into the reference slide's stain space.
⑤ 反轉到 RGBBack to RGB I_norm = I_0 · exp(−OD_norm)，得到正規化影像。I_norm = I_0 · exp(−OD_norm) returns the normalized image.
為何 Macenko 必須在 OD 空間？因為 stain 透光率呈指數，在 OD 空間線性。RGB 空間直接 PCA 會給錯誤 stain vector。
Why must Macenko run in OD space? Because stain transmittance is exponential — it linearizes in OD. Running PCA directly in RGB yields wrong stain vectors.
思辨討論

數位病理三大爭論

A. 為什麼跨醫院顏色差異殺死 DL 模型？
你的模型在訓練醫院 AUC 0.95，部署到另一家醫院剩 0.65。為什麼？
Your model has AUC 0.95 at the training hospital, drops to 0.65 elsewhere. Why?
提示：H&E 染色配方、機器、抹片厚度差異 → 顏色分佈大不同。DL 看顏色多於形狀。Macenko 正規化或 StainGAN 必做。
Hint: H&E recipes, machines, and section thicknesses differ → colour distributions diverge. DL relies on colour more than shape. Always Macenko-normalize or use StainGAN.
B. UNI 與 Cellpose 在病理 pipeline 順序？
兩者用途不同，但在同一研究怎麼用？
Their uses differ — how do they coexist in one study?
提示：Cellpose / HoVer-Net 做 nucleus instance segmentation；UNI 抽 patch embedding → CLAM MIL 做 slide-level cancer 分類。兩條 path 並行。
Hint: Cellpose / HoVer-Net for nucleus instance; UNI patch embedding → CLAM MIL for slide-level cancer classification. Two parallel paths.
C. 弱監督 MIL 需要多少 slide？
<100 slide 訓練 CLAM 為何不穩？
Why is CLAM training unstable with <100 slides?
提示：MIL 每個 bag 只 1 label，需要很多 bags 才能學到 stable attention。<100 slides 改用 foundation model 抽 embedding + linear classifier 更穩。
Hint: each bag has just 1 label; MIL needs many bags to learn stable attention. With <100 slides, use FM embeddings + a linear classifier for stability.
精選案例

兩個改寫病理的故事

① QuPath：一個人寫的全球病理工具
Bankhead et al., Sci. Rep. 7:16878 (2017) · qupath.github.io

Peter Bankhead 2017 在 Queen's University Belfast 為了博士論文寫 QuPath，原本只給自己用。Sci. Rep. 2017 論文被引 6,000+ 次，QuPath 成為全球 10,000+ 病理學家的標配。它的 Groovy scripting + viewer 整合，把「IHC 量化」從 ImageJ macro 解放出來，又支援 multiplex IF / CyCIF。2025 年 QuPath 仍是病理開源軟體 #1。

Peter Bankhead wrote QuPath at Queen's University Belfast (2017) — initially for his own PhD work. The Sci. Rep. 2017 paper has 6,000+ citations; QuPath is now the workhorse for 10,000+ pathologists globally. Its Groovy scripting + viewer freed 'IHC quantitation' from ImageJ macros and supports multiplex IF / CyCIF. As of 2025, QuPath is the #1 open-source pathology software.

啟示：一個人的 PhD project 也可能變成整個領域的工具。
Lesson: a single PhD project can become a field's standard tool.
② UNI：100M patch 訓出病理通用嵌入
Chen et al., Nat. Medicine 30:850 (2024) · Mahmood Lab DFCI

Richard Chen 等在 Dana-Farber Mahmood Lab 用 DINOv2 訓練 UNI：~100M 病理 patches、ViT-L backbone、自監督預訓練。在 ~30 個下游任務（cancer 分類、生物標誌預測、prognosis 預測）平均超越 ImageNet pretrained baseline 約 20%。UNI 2 (2025) 進一步擴展到 200k slides，重塑整個 computational pathology。

Richard Chen et al. (DFCI Mahmood Lab) trained UNI with DINOv2: ~100M pathology patches, ViT-L backbone, self-supervised. On ~30 downstream tasks (cancer classification, biomarker prediction, prognosis) it beats ImageNet baselines by ~20% on average. UNI 2 (2025) extended to 200k slides, reshaping computational pathology entirely.

啟示：在「特殊視覺領域」自訓 foundation model 仍有顯著價值。
Lesson: in 'specialized visual domains,' training a dedicated foundation model still pays.
