去卷積 (Deconvolution)

顯微鏡會用「點擴散函數 (PSF)」模糊真實樣本。去卷積在數學上反轉這個模糊，讓影像更銳利、更可量化——3D widefield 與共軛焦尤其有感。

A microscope blurs reality by its point-spread function (PSF). Deconvolution inverts that blur mathematically — sharper, more quantitative images, especially in 3D widefield and confocal.

開場故事 · STORY

1972 年的數學如何救活每一張共軛焦

1972 年 William Richardson 與 1974 年 Leon Lucy 各自獨立發表了同一個迭代去模糊法——前者是光學家，後者是天文學家，兩人為了星體影像而非顯微鏡。後人合稱 Richardson-Lucy decon。

半世紀後，這個演算法仍在 widefield、共軛焦、light-sheet 上日復一日地使用。把模糊「逆回去」聽起來像魔法，但本質是 PSF 的數學建模 + Poisson 最大概似估計。2018 CARE 用 CNN 跳脫顯式 PSF 後，整個領域進入 DL 時代——但 RL 的數學直覺仍是必修。

In 1972, William Richardson — and Leon Lucy in 1974 — independently published the same iterative deblurring method. Neither was a microscopist; one was an optician, one an astronomer. We now call it Richardson-Lucy deconvolution.

Half a century later it still runs daily on widefield, confocal, and light-sheet stacks. 'Reversing blur' sounds magical, but it's just PSF modelling + Poisson MLE. After CARE (2018) added CNNs to bypass explicit PSFs, the field went DL — but RL's intuition is still required reading.

🤔 為什麼 RL 在 30 次迭代後 MSE 開始上升？這背後揭露了「迭代式還原」的核心 trade-off。
🤔 Why does RL's MSE start rising after ~30 iterations? It exposes the core trade-off of any iterative restoration.
觀念

一、PSF 與影像形成模型

點擴散函數 (Point Spread Function, PSF)：一個亞解析度的光點（如小於繞射極限的螢光珠）在顯微鏡下不會仍是「一個點」，而會擴散成一個有特定形狀的模糊斑——這個斑就是 PSF。

影像形成的線性模型：

image = sample ⊗ PSF + noise

(⊗ 表示卷積)。去卷積就是已知 PSF（測量或理論），反推 sample。

The Point Spread Function (PSF) is the image of a sub-resolution point (e.g., a sub-diffraction-limited fluorescent bead) — it appears not as a point but as a blurred spot with a characteristic shape. That spot is the PSF.

Linear image-formation model:

image = sample ⊗ PSF + noise

(⊗ denotes convolution). Deconvolution recovers sample from a known PSF (measured or theoretical).

💡
關鍵直覺：PSF 在 XY 方向通常是 Gaussian-like，但在 Z 方向是不對稱的「沙漏 / 雙錐」形狀。這也是為什麼 3D 去卷積比 2D 重要得多——3D widefield 的離焦光是訊號的主要敵人。 Key intuition: The PSF is roughly Gaussian in XY but asymmetric ("hourglass / double cone") in Z. That is why 3D decon matters far more than 2D — in 3D widefield, out-of-focus light is the dominant enemy.

決策

三、要不要去卷積？

🌳 去卷積決策樹

Q1:
顯微術式？→ Widefield 3D：強烈建議。共軛焦：建議。Light-sheet：強烈建議 (multi-view 融合)。STED / SIM：通常不需。
Q2:
有 sub-resolution bead 影像可量 PSF 嗎？→ 有 → 用測量 PSF（最準）。沒有 → 用 PSFGenerator 算理論 PSF（Born-Wolf / Gibson-Lanni）
Q3:
要做強度量化嗎？→ 要 → 慎用！RL 不保線性，建議改用 Wiener（線性）或加 TV 正則
Q4:
有大量訓練配對資料嗎？→ 有 → CARE / DL decon，效果最好；沒有 → RL-TV 是黃金標準
Q1:
Modality? → Widefield 3D: strongly recommended. Confocal: recommended. Light-sheet: strongly recommended for multi-view fusion. STED/SIM: usually not needed.
Q2:
Bead images for PSF measurement available? → Yes → use measured PSF. No → compute theoretical PSF (Born-Wolf / Gibson-Lanni) via PSFGenerator.
Q3:
Will you quantify intensity? → Yes → caution! RL is not linearity-preserving; consider Wiener (linear) or RL with TV regularization.
Q4:
Lots of paired training data? → Yes → CARE / DL decon, best results. No → RL-TV is the gold standard.
⚠️
順序很重要：在 widefield，必須先做背景減除（移除離焦光的低頻成分）再做去卷積。否則離焦光會被「銳化」成偽訊號。 Order matters: In widefield, subtract background first (remove out-of-focus low-frequency content), then deconvolve. Otherwise the haze gets "sharpened" into pseudo-signal.

公式拆解

Richardson-Lucy 迭代公式

Σ 逐步推導
x(k+1) = x(k) · [PSFT ⊛ (image / (PSF ⊛ x(k)))]
① 初始估計Initial guess x⁰ 通常取 = image 或 = mean(image)。x⁰ usually = image or = mean(image).
② 正向模型Forward model PSF ⊛ x^(k) = 用目前估計合成的「理論影像」。PSF ⊛ x^(k) = synthetic 'theoretical image' from the current estimate.
③ 比值Ratio image / (PSF ⊛ x^(k))：與實際影像比較，得到「需要放大多少」的因子。image / (PSF ⊛ x^(k)): compare to the real image to get a 'how much to scale' factor.
④ 反向投影Back-projection PSF^T ⊛ ratio：把因子按 PSF 對稱反向卷積回去。PSF^T ⊛ ratio: convolve the factor backward through PSF.
⑤ 更新Update 乘法更新：x^(k+1) = x^(k) × 因子。Poisson MLE 的 EM 更新規則。Multiplicative update: x^(k+1) = x^(k) × factor. The EM rule for Poisson MLE.
早期迭代 MSE 下降，late iterations noise 放大成 ringing。經驗法則：10–30 iter；自動停止可用 MSE 拐點偵測或 SURE 估計。
Early iterations lower MSE; late iterations amplify noise into ringing. Rule of thumb: 10–30 iter; auto-stop via MSE plateau detection or SURE estimation.
思辨討論

去卷積三大爭論

A. 量化還能在 decon 後做嗎？
你的 GFP-FRET 螢光強度需要 ±5% 準確度。RL decon 不保線性，可以用嗎？
Your GFP-FRET intensity needs ±5% accuracy. RL decon isn't linearity-preserving — can you use it?
提示：RL 不保強度線性；想量化用 Wiener（線性）或 RL-TV（受控）。對所有條件用相同設定 + 同一 PSF。
Hint: RL isn't linearity-preserving; use Wiener (linear) or RL-TV (controlled). Use identical settings and the same PSF across conditions.
B. 理論 PSF 何時失敗？
你用 60× 油鏡（n=1.515）拍水樣本（n=1.33）深 50 μm。Born-Wolf 還能信嗎？
60× oil (n=1.515) imaging an aqueous sample (n=1.33) at 50 μm depth — can you still trust Born-Wolf?
提示：折射率失配 → 球差。深樣本必須改用 Gibson-Lanni 或 bead-measured PSF。
Hint: refractive-index mismatch → spherical aberration. Switch to Gibson-Lanni or bead-measured PSFs for deep tissue.
C. CARE 在訓練分布外表現？
你的 CARE 模型用 fixed HeLa 訓練。拿來推論 live cardiomyocytes 會怎樣？
Your CARE model was trained on fixed HeLa. What happens when you infer on live cardiomyocytes?
提示：DL 模型對 OOD 樣本可能「幻想」結構。永遠在新 sample 做 validation；保留 raw 影像作雙重檢查。
Hint: DL models can 'hallucinate' structure on OOD samples. Always validate on new samples; keep raw as a control.
精選案例

兩個改寫去卷積歷史的故事

① CARE：DL 第一次擊敗傳統 decon
Weigert et al., Nat Methods 15:1090 (2018) · DOI: 10.1038/s41592-018-0216-7

2018 年 Martin Weigert 等在 Dresden 提出 CARE：用 noisy / high-quality 配對訓練 CNN 直接學還原映射。不需顯式 PSF，且能同時 denoise + deconvolve + super-resolve。在 planaria 與 flatworm 影像上達到「5× 短曝光、同等畫質」。CARE 從此改變生物影像：你可以拍得更暗、活更久、看更深。

In 2018 Martin Weigert and colleagues at Dresden published CARE: train a CNN on noisy/clean pairs to learn the restoration map directly. No explicit PSF needed, and it could denoise + deconvolve + super-resolve simultaneously. On planaria they reached '5× shorter exposure for the same quality.' CARE changed bioimaging — you can now image dimmer, longer, deeper.

啟示：當你不知道精確的 PSF，「資料驅動」往往比「物理建模」更實用。
Lesson: when the exact PSF is unknown, data-driven often beats physics-driven.
② Light-sheet 多視角融合：從 6 視角到一張各向同性 3D
Preibisch / Hörl BigStitcher · DOI: 10.1038/s41592-019-0501-0

BigStitcher 把多視角光片資料 (例如從 6 個角度拍胚胎) 進行 multi-view fusion + 去卷積。原本各向異性的單視角 → 重建出近乎 isotropic 的 3D。Mette Boye 在 EMBL Heidelberg 用 BigStitcher 拍下整隻 fly 胚胎發育，每張影像 200 GB，全程用 BigStitcher fusion + decon，輸出供下游 Cellpose 3D 分割。

BigStitcher fuses light-sheet data acquired from many angles (e.g., 6 views of an embryo) and deconvolves jointly. The originally anisotropic single views → a near-isotropic 3-D reconstruction. Mette Boye at EMBL Heidelberg used BigStitcher to image whole fly embryo development — 200 GB per snapshot, all fused + deconvolved with BigStitcher, then fed into Cellpose 3D.

啟示：多視角 + 去卷積結合，是當前最強的 3D 影像重建組合。
Lesson: multi-view + decon is the strongest 3-D reconstruction combo we have today.
